About Me

Welcome to my online porfolio. My name is Sherif Fattal, I'm an aspiring developer from Cairo, Egypt. I grew up in Cairo all my life and moved to the US for undergrad in Boston where I attended Bentley University, and then New York post-graduation. At Bentley I pursued a degree in management, specifically supply chain management. This path led me to my first job at a shipping and logisitcs startup called wynd, and shortly after I joined the founding team of a prescription delivery startup called MedsNow. The exposure I've had to the startup scene is what peaked my interest in web development, because I constantly felt a need to execute on the ideas I have, but lacked the technical skills to do so.

Outside of work I'm an avid gamer, shooters and mmorpgs are my favorite to play. I love all genres of music, a few of my top bands are Pink Floyd. I'm also a huge Liverpool FC fan (Up the Reds!) - that's football, though you probably refer to it as "soccer".

I hope you enjoy my portfolio. Feel free to send me a message through the contact form if you have any questions, inquiries.
